# Glossary

Status: ACTIVE

## Marketsynth

Official product name.

## Source of Truth

Approved architecture and governance layer defining project truth.

## Runtime

The governed execution environment where tasks, approvals, evidence and outcomes are tracked.

## Knowledge Candidate

A proposed knowledge artifact derived from evidence and outcomes. Not automatically global.

## Human Approval

Explicit human decision allowing real execution.

## Readiness

Technical and quality state indicating that an artifact may be eligible for approval.

## Tenant

Isolated customer/workspace boundary.
